About Me

Since launching my career in 2021, post-COVID lockdown, I’ve had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of businesses—from independent freelancers to creative agencies and marketing teams. These experiences, combined with my educational background—a B.A. in Digital Media with a concentration in Web Design from the University of Central Florida—have helped me grow into a well-rounded web design professional.

I’m particularly passionate about creating efficient workflows; building clean, accessible websites; and providing support for my team wherever needed. Outside of work, you’ll often find me spending quality time with friends and family, trying out artsy projects, or joining Expedition 33 in the open world of Clair Obscura (10/10 would recommend for anyone who plays video games).
